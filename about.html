Who We Are

Principal Architect:Ar.A.Bhuvaneshwari Graduated from the School of Architecture and planning, Anna University, Guindy, Chennai and also did her Master of Architecture in Habitat Design from KSOA. After graduation, Later by 3rd December 2004 she established her firm Modern Space Architects at Tirunelveli. Her initial practices were more into alternative techniques. Lack of field staffs made her to visit sites more often and it also groomed her practice as a more personalized one and her approach to clients became more humane. Over the past sixteen years her practices has pioneered cost effective, climate responsive and sustainable over wide range of projects. Her practice had penetrated into remote villages of Tamil Nadu and her intelligence can be felt straightly in these region like Tirunelveli district, Thoothukudi district, Tenkasi district, Kanyakumari district.
